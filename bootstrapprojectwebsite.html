Frequently Asked Questions

We do not have a physical office, rather we are comprised of a team of seasoned industry developers who teach and instruct remotely.

You can either pay in full for the 4 month bootcamp which is $3,250. We also offer the option for a month by month payment of $812.

You will be taught the essential web technologies starting off with HTML5, CSS3, and other related technologies such as CSS Grid and Bootstrap. We then will transition into our first real programming language, Javascript. We will finish the course teaching you the most popular Javascript frameworks such as React and Vue. You will be assigned projects throughout the course to put your knowledge together and apply it.

Check to see which bootcamp schedule is right for you. We offer flexible scheduling as we have various instructors who teach remotely. Once you find the preferred bootcamp start date, register and create your account and pick your payment option.

While we cannot 100% guarantee anything, around 90% of our students get accepted for their first junior dev role within 6 months of completion of our bootcamp. We provide all the essential technologies and projects to develop your portfolio so your chance of hire is very strong.
